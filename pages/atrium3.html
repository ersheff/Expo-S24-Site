Know Your Neighbor

Jason Allen, J Scherrer, Connor Zielinski

Project Description

Explore a twenty-four hour snapshot of three characters' lives, all playing out in front of you at the exact same time. "Know Your Neighbor" is designed to be played by rotating one's monitor vertically, so that you can peer into the apartment complex these characters live in. When trauma comes knocking at your door, it's always helpful to know your neighbor!
