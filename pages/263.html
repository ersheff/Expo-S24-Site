Love & Hangover

Brie Merritt

Project Description

These three canvases are made up of beer cans, beer bottle caps, and wine corks found around Oxford, Ohio in alleyways behind restaurants and bars and in front of fraternity houses. The two portraits represent Miami University's student body and how drinking culture impacts how they are portrayed on the outside. In the third canvas, the Miami University logo is in the center, surrounded by cans to represent how alcohol impacts the campus community. I want my pieces to bring awareness to how drinking can affect peoples' behavior and blind them to how their actions negatively affect Oxford's environment.

Love and Honor, On Paper

Luke Finley

Project Description

Love and Honor, On Paper is a protest to the hypocrisy behind Miami University and their slogan: "Love and Honor." This project discusses in detail the issues that Miami University has perpetuated regarding professor treatment: primarily of Adjunct and VAP professors.
