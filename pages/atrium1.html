Tales of Italia

Loreweaver Games

Leo Nicholson, Garland Weaver

Project Description

A new TTRPG inspired by the Italian traditions of Commedia Dell'Arte in which you and your fellow players must take on the roles of the Commedia's classic stock characters through a randomized scenario guided by Tarot Cards. The GM/Storyteller will receive random plot points from the major arcana, whilst the success of your actions is determined by the minor arcana. Designed for 2, 4, or 6 players and one GM.
